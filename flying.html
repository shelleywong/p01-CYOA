☼ The way of the phoenix... ☀

 

After flying for miles, the phoenix starts to head back toward land. She finds a bit of shelter from the rain, creates a spark, and then a fire. You start enjoying the warmth, before you realize what’s happening… “It is time,” she says, as her feathers start to blacken. You are frozen in shock, and; although not in the fire, the smoke surrounds you, and you soon cannot breath. You are dead.
