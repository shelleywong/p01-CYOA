☼ Questing forward... ☀

 

When you hear rushing water, you know you are close to the river at the far end of the forest. “We’re almost there, Sweetie!” You head for the section where you usually pass, but the current storm has made the waves stronger and the river much deeper. You pause to get a sense of the situation. In that moment, Sweetie shouts, “we can swim across!” and before you can stop her, she’s in the water. You jump in after her, but as you reach her, you feel a rush of water overtake both of you. You are dead.
