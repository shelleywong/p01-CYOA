☼ Answering the door... ☀

 

When you open the door, you find a stallion with a moustache and a grave look on his face. “The Evil Asp is active again,” he says, “he has conjured a storm system which he intends to maintain for all eternity! It is the beginning of his scheme to rule all of Equestra, and you, Firefly, are the only one who can stop him! The secret to his power lies in the Ruby Gem, hidden near Tornado Town.” You know that getting to Tornado Town will be a significant journey. The strengthening storm makes it difficult to fly with any accuracy.
