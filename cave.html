☼ Shelter in the cave... ☀

 

“Hello?” You hear yourself echoing through the cave. “Is anyone in here?” A giant, hairy form slowly stomps toward you. You are about to run away, but at that moment, you hear him grunt and notice that he will not stop staring at you. With no other options in mind, you start walking farther back into the cave to find a place to rest. But then you feel a rumble. Earthquake! Before you can think twice, a giant rock hits you in the head and you blackout. You are dead.
