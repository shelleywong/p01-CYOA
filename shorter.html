☼ The shorter mountain path... ☀

 

The closer you get to the mountain, the more it looks like a pyramid- dauntingly steep and slippery. But you press on, hoping to find some shelter from the rain and wind amid the dense pine trees. As the path plateaus and you notice a small clearing amid the trees, you look around to see where to head next.
