☼ Toward the station... ☀

 

Galloping toward the station, you spy something red atop the rails. It’s the ruby gem! You sprint toward it, knowing that when it’s in your possession, you can gain the power necessary to fight the Evil Asp! In your excitement, and with the loud winds whipping around, you don’t hear the sound of a train coming up on you. When you finally notice it, you try to hurry away, but can’t move. Your hoof is stuck, and the train cannot stop. You are dead.
