☼ Branch hopping... ☀

 

You’re hopping from branch to branch, excited that you seem to making good time. You’ll have saved the Ruby Gem in no time! You speed up a bit, but in your excitement, you don’t notice the branch you’re heading to already has an occupant: a nest of wasps! Before you can change directions, you’ve hit the nest, and an angry swarm surrounds you. Knowing that you are allergic to wasp venom, you curse yourself for not being more careful. All you were thinking about was your loyalty to your pony friends and kin! But soon you are overtaken by anaphylactic shock. You are dead.
