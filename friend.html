☼ Help for a friend... ☀

 

“AAHHH!!!” You see Twinkle Toes fall, so you hitch up your bootstraps and sprint toward her. She has tripped and fallen, and she looks injured. As you get near, you slow down and stoop toward her. “Lean on me!” You both start to struggle back to town, through the open field adjacent to Ponyvale. You see a flash of light, and almost immediately hear loud thunder. You know you have to hurry. “Almost there!” You know you’re getting close, because you’re nearing the apple grove just outside town. But as you start running through the trees, you see a flash of light. You are dead.
