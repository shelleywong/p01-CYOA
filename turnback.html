☼ Get Sweetie home... ☀

 

“This detour will be worth it,” you think to yourself, “if it means ensuring that Sweetie Blossom is safe.” You’re making good time, when all of a sudden, you see a dark figure in front of you. It’s a grizzly bear, and a couple little cubs behind her! Sweetie grabs a broken branch and steps forward. “NO!” you yell, and try to stop her, but the sudden forward movement was all it took to excite the mother bear. The attack happens before you can react. You are dead.
