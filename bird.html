You quickly catch up to the phoenix and ask if they know what’s going on. Storms rarely occur in Ponyvale, especially not in summertime! “It is the work of the Evil Asp,” she says, “you must turn back! Weather patrol is the first line of defense against him. You can be the catalyst for good that your community needs!”
